About Us

We are passionate about farming and we take pride in the fruits and vegetables we grow. We use only the highest standards of organic growing practices and food safety. Our produce is grown locally so it is vine ripened before it is picked and therefore it tastes better. When you buy our fruits and vegetables you are supporting a local company that hires local employees and gives back to the community. Come visit us and see for yourself!

We guarantee you that you will fine a wide variety of different fruits that will meet your expectations. We boast a 20tn apple harvesting. We grow a variaty of apples in our farms using the least quantity of chemical pesticides possible. However, we should not ignore the vegetables that we grow. We own a 20k square meters farm awash with tomatoes, cucumbers, eggplants etc. Please check "Our Produce" and find more about the products that we retail.

We also provide you with food transport solutions. This entails that we can bring the fruits and the vegetables safely at your facilities. You can find out more about this service in our "Order" menu.
